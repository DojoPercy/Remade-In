# 📚 Remade In — Content Editor Guide

Welcome to your website's content editor! This guide explains how to make changes to your website without needing a developer.

---

## 🎯 What Can You Edit?

You can edit everything that appears on your website:

### **🏠 Home Page**
The main page visitors see when they arrive. This includes:
- **Hero Banner** — The big eye-catching section at the top with headline, image, and buttons
- **Scrolling Message** — The ticker that loops text across the page
- **We Are The Glue** — Your problem/solution section with 3 key points
- **Impact Stats** — Numbers showing your impact (water saved, CO₂ avoided, etc.)
- **Support Banner** — The donation/partnership call-to-action at the bottom

### **⚙️ Website Settings**
Global information that appears on every page:
- **Logo & Branding** — Your logo and tagline
- **Navigation Menu** — Links in the header
- **Social Media Links** — Where people can follow you
- **Contact Information** — Email and footer text

### **📚 Collections** (You can add, edit, and delete these)
- **Research Archive** — Reports, white papers, policy briefs
- **Community Voices** — Stories and testimonials
- **Gallery** — Photos from events and work in the field
- **Partners** — Organizations you work with

### **📖 Blueprint Page**
The separate page with your blueprint or white paper resources.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Log in** to your content editor at: `studio.yoursite.com`
2. **You'll see a menu** on the left with all the sections you can edit
3. **Click any section** to open it and make changes
4. **Click "Save"** when you're done

---

## 📝 How to Edit the Home Page

### **Step 1: Open Home Page**
1. In the left menu, click **"🏠 Home Page"**
2. You'll see tabs at the top: "🎯 Hero Banner", "📜 Scrolling Message", etc.
3. Click the tab for the section you want to edit

### **Step 2: Make Your Changes**
Each field has a **label** (what to edit) and a **description** (how to edit it).

Example:
```
Main Headline
The first part of the big headline. 
Example: "Building our Circular Fashion"
```

### **Step 3: Save Your Changes**
Click **"Publish"** or **"Save Draft"** in the top-right corner.

---

## 📖 Understanding Field Types

### **Text Field** ✏️
Simple text. Example: "Our Impact"

### **Paragraph/Text Block** 📄
Multiple lines of text. Example: Your mission statement.

### **Image** 🖼️
Upload a photo. Always add **Alt Text** (a short description) for accessibility.

### **Number** 🔢
Used for stats, pages, or ordering. Example: `500000`

### **Link** 🔗
Full URL address. Must start with `https://`

### **Checkbox** ☑️
Toggle something on/off. Examples:
- ☑️ Show on Website
- ☑️ Feature This Document

### **Select List** 📋
Choose from options provided. Example:
- Report
- White Paper
- Policy Brief

## 🔄 How to Manage Collections

### **To Add a New Item**
1. In the left menu, click the collection name (e.g., "Community Voices")
2. Click **"Create"** or **"New"** button
3. Fill in the fields
4. Click **"Publish"**

### **To Edit an Existing Item**
1. Click the collection name in the left menu
2. Find the item in the list
3. Click it to open
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Publish"**

### **To Delete an Item**
1. Open the item
2. Click **"Delete"** (usually in a menu or at the bottom)
3. Confirm deletion

### **To Reorder Items**
Most collections have an "Order" or "Display Order" field:
- Use numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4, etc.
- Lower numbers appear first
- Re-publish to apply the new order

## 🖼️ Gallery Tips

Your gallery has 5 spots arranged like this:

```
┌─────────────────────┬──────────┐
│   a (Large)         │  b       │
│                     │(Portrait)│
├────────┬────────┬───┴──────────┤
│   c    │   d    │      e       │
│(Small) │(Small) │ (Wide)       │
└────────┴────────┘──────────────┘
```

**Tips:**
- Choose the right spot (a, b, c, d, or e) for each image
- If multiple images go in the same spot, use the "Order" field to choose which one shows
- Use "Focal Position" to control which part of the photo is visible (center, top, left, etc.)
- Toggle "Show on Website" to hide an image temporarily without deleting it

## 🔗 Navigation Menu

This is the menu at the top of your website.

To change it:
1. Go to **⚙️ Website Settings**
2. Scroll to **📍 Navigation Menu**
3. Add, edit, or remove links
4. Use "Order" numbers to reorder (1, 2, 3...)

**Link Examples:**
- `/about` — Links to a page on your site
- `/impact#stats` — Links to a section with an anchor
- `https://example.com` — Links to an external website

---

## 💬 Social Links

To add your social media accounts:
1. Go to **⚙️ Website Settings**
2. Scroll to **💬 Social & Contact**
3. Click **"Add"** in the Social Links section
4. Select the platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
5. Paste the full URL to your profile
6. Example: `https://instagram.com/yourhandle`

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### **Required Fields** (marked with 🔴)
These MUST be filled in. You can't publish without them.

### **Don't Delete Critical Documents**
These appear on every page — be careful!
- Home Page
- Website Settings

### **Dates & Years**
- Format: `YYYY-MM-DD` (example: `2025-03-19`)
- Or use the date picker that appears

### **File Uploads**
- PDFs should be optimized (under 10MB if possible)
- Images should be at least 1200px wide for quality
- Always add "Alt Text" to images

---

## 🔍 What Happens When You Publish?

When you click **"Publish"**, your changes go live on the website immediately.

**Draft Mode:** You can save changes as a draft without publishing. Drafts are only visible to editors, not to visitors.

---

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: Can I undo changes?**
Yes! Most systems keep a version history. Your developer can help you revert changes if needed.

### **Q: What if I make a mistake?**
Don't worry! Edit the content again and publish the correction. Or save it as a draft and ask your developer to review before publishing.

### **Q: Can I preview changes before publishing?**
Yes! Use the **"Preview"** button to see how changes look on the website before making them live.

### **Q: How long do changes take to appear?**
Usually instantly. Sometimes up to 1 minute.

### **Q: Can I edit deleted items?**
Deleted items cannot be recovered. Always confirm deletion carefully.

## 📞 Need Help?

If something doesn't work:
1. **Try refreshing** the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. **Check all "Required" fields** are filled
3. **Look at the error message** — it often explains the problem
4. **Contact your developer** if you're stuck
